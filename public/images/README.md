# How to Add Your Photo

## Steps:

1. **Add your photo** to this folder (`public/images/`)
2. **Rename it** to `profile.jpg` or `profile.png`
3. The portfolio will automatically use it!

## Recommended Photo Specifications:

- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Size:** 500x500 pixels (square)
- **Quality:** High resolution
- **Background:** Professional or plain background
- **Lighting:** Good lighting, clear face
- **File Size:** Under 500KB for fast loading

## Alternative Names Supported:

- `profile.jpg`
- `profile.png`
- `avatar.jpg`
- `avatar.png`
- `photo.jpg`
- `photo.png`

Just drop your photo file here and the portfolio will display it!
